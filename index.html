About

Jahe Social Scheduler is a creator-focused platform that helps users plan, review, and schedule their TikTok content in a structured and user-controlled workflow.

How It Works

  1. Connect your TikTok account securely via authorization
  2. Upload and prepare your content
  3. Review captions and content before publishing
  4. Select your preferred publishing time
  5. Publish content with full user control
